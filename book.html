The Book

Written from Lived Experience, Not Just Theory

After thirty-four years in nursing — sixteen as a clinical nurse specialist on an Intensive Care Unit — Gill knows what chronic stress looks like from the inside out. She has lived through burnout, breakdown, and the long road back to herself.

Stress Less? Hell Yes! is the book she wished she'd had during her darkest times. It's honest, practical, and written with the warmth of someone who truly understands what you're going through.

This isn't another book telling you to “just relax” whilst your nervous system is stuck in survival mode. It's a compassionate, straight-to-the-point guide for anyone who is overwhelmed, burnt out, or constantly under pressure — and ready to find their way back to feeling calm and in control.

Gill M Edwards is a former NHS clinical nurse specialist turned Stress Disruptor Coach.
