Community

A Place Where Everyone Belongs

Have you ever been told you can't sing? Has someone told you to stop 'catawalling'? Were you ever asked to just mime?

Then this group is especially for you.

I started the Singalong group in my hometown of Stoke-on-Trent because I believe wholeheartedly that everyone can sing. Singing brings joy, connection, and a sense of belonging that's hard to find elsewhere. It's also one of the most powerful natural stress relievers there is.

This isn't a choir. There are no auditions. No one is judging your voice. This is about coming together, having fun, and experiencing the pure joy of singing — together.

Singalong Sessions

What to Expect

Everyone Welcome

Anyone and everyone are welcome. No experience needed. No talent required. Just bring yourself and a willingness to have fun.

No Judgement

This is a safe, supportive space. Whether you're a shower singer or a secret soprano, you belong here.

Pure Joy

We sing because it feels good. Science tells us singing releases endorphins, reduces stress, and boosts your immune system. But mostly, it's just brilliant fun.

Community Connection

Meet wonderful people, make friends, and be part of something that lifts everyone up. Singing together creates bonds that go beyond words.
